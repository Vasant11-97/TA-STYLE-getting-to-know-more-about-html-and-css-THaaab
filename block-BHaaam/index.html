1. Chole Bhature.

-Chole Bhature is a Indian dish. The origin of this dish is in the nothern part of India but it is loved in whole India. This dish is made up of chickpeas and fullfy breads. Every part of India has different ways to enjoy this dish. In Northern India it is enjoyed in morning as a breakfast while in other parts it is served as a lunch or dinner. The rich flavours of this dish makes it very beautiful and tasty.
-Chole is prepared by pressure cooking chickpeas and adding spices such as cumin, coriander seeds, turmeric powder, and chili powder. Onion, garlic, and ginger are also added for additional flavor. Bhature is prepared by combining flour, salt, and oil, and kneading the dough. The dough is rolled out into circles and deep fried until the bhature puff up.
-A favourite dish in Northern India, and also popular as street food in Delhi, especially in the winter season, it is liked in all corners of the country. It may be prepared with slight changes here and there, but what comes out at the end, hot steaming poories and the tangy tasty Chole, which can be made like gravy to almost dry but hot and spicy type, based on likes and preferences in pockets or even a region.

2. Pasta

Pastas are divided into two broad categories: dried (pasta secca) and fresh (pasta fresca). Most dried pasta is produced commercially via an extrusion process, although it can be produced at home. Fresh pasta is traditionally produced by hand, sometimes with the aid of simple machines.[3] Fresh pastas available in grocery stores are produced commercially by large-scale machines.
Both dried and fresh pastas come in a number of shapes and varieties, with 310 specific forms known by over 1300 documented names.[4] In Italy, the names of specific pasta shapes or types often vary by locale. For example, the pasta form cavatelli is known by 28 different names depending upon the town and region. Common forms of pasta include long and short shapes, tubes, flat shapes or sheets, miniature shapes for soup, those meant to be filled or stuffed, and specialty or decorative shapes.
